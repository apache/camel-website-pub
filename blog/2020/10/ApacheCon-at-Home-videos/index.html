Recordings from ApacheCon @Home 2020

If you missed some of the talks on the Camel/Integration or would like to revisit your favorites, video recordings from ApacheCon @Home 2020 are now available on The Apache Software Foundation YouTube channel.
Posted on October 19, 2020, by

Share this blog

❮ ❯

This year’s ApacheCon was an overwhelming success, with many tracks running in parallel. If you missed some of the talks on the Camel/Integration or would like to revisit your favorites, video recordings from ApacheCon @Home 2020 are now available on The Apache Software Foundation YouTube channel.

You can watch the content from all three days in one playlist, with over 10 hours of content.

For convenience, we listed the talks as they appear in the schedule here along with the slides shared by the speakers.

Camel at ApacheCon @Home 2020

 July 1, 2020, by María Arias de Reyna, Zoran Regvart

We are pleased to announce that ApacheCon 2020 will be held online, September 29th through October 1st, 2020. If you wish to present at the event please submit your talk proposal for the Camel/Integration track when at ApacheCon 2020 website no later than Monday, July 13th by noon in the UTC timezone. Please do not wait for the last minute to submit. We are most interested to see talks that offer a learning experience to the attendees, so talks that present new parts of the Camel ecosystem (Camel K, Camel Quarkus, Camel Kafka Connector), talks showing off lessons learned, use cases, and visions on where software integration is heading in the future.
